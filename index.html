About Us

Amaatra, a Sanskrit word, literally means ‘limitless’.

Amaatra CRYSTAL is a Joint venture Project of AMAATRA GROUP and SB GROUP.

Amaatra Crystal is an upcoming Residential Project placed at Plot No. GH-02, Jaipuria Sunrise Township, NH 24 , Ghaziabad (UP) which includes all the facilities and amenities.

The Project offers 2BHK,3BHK and 4BHK apartments with the sizes ranging from 1045 Sq.ft. to 2525 Sq.ft. available for all residential buyers.

The Township spreads over 30 acres of land with lots of green area and a superlative design.
